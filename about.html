About

We are passionate about lowering pricing at Rasvel's. We are constantly looking for ways to ensure that you may enjoy your life regardless of your budget.

Rasvel's,  launched in 2019, has always been committed to providing the finest low-price shopping experience possible by delivering excellent products at low costs in a bright, easy-to-navigate store with helpful employees. Today, Rasvel's proudly operates over 8 stores across GTA.
